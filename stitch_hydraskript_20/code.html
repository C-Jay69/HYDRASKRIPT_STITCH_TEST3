System Actions Reporting

The narrator should report system actions (dice rolls, failures) as part of the internal monologue.

"The lock clicked, but not because of skill. A lucky roll of the bones, a nat 20 in a world of zeros."
Style: Prose close

Metaphorical Weather

The environment must always reflect the character's internal misery or cynicism.

"The rain didn't wash away the filth; it just turned the city into a giant, wet tomb."
Style: Dialogue close

Short Sentence Structure

Limit complex sentences. Use periods like bullets.

"She walked in. I looked up. The world ended. Just like that."
